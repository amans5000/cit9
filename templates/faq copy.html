What is CIT?
CIT (Creativity & Innovation Test) is a national-level competition designed to test logical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation.
Where will the test be conducted?
The test will be conducted completely online and can be attempted from anywhere.
How will the test be conducted?
The competition consists of multiple rounds with MCQs and analytical challenges.
How will the competition be structured?
CIT is structured in elimination rounds, with each round testing higher-order skills.
Is there any prize money?
Yes, winners will receive exciting prizes, certificates, and recognition.
How will it benefit me?
Participants gain national exposure, certificates, skill validation, and learning experience.
